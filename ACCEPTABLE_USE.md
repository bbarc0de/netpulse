# Acceptable use policy draft

> **Requires qualified legal review.** This operational draft does not create a complete legal safe harbor or enforcement promise.

Do not use NetPulse to:

- exhaust bandwidth, evade rate limits, coordinate denial-of-service traffic, or automate repeated tests without written authorization;
- submit fabricated, duplicated, harassing, discriminatory, unlawful, or impersonated outage/provider reports;
- probe or access systems, accounts, data, or endpoints without authorization;
- upload or transmit credentials, personal data, malware, tracking links, or secrets through notes or reports;
- reverse engineer private infrastructure credentials or bypass Turnstile, WAF, quotas, or signed-operation controls;
- present NetPulse results as proof that the project has not actually established.

Security research should follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Operators may throttle, block, remove, or investigate activity needed to protect the service while seeking to avoid unnecessary impact on accessibility tools and privacy-conscious users.
